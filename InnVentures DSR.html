Section 02  /  Business Case + Proposed Solution

The Problem You're Solving — and How Reeco Closes It.

Four things slowing your team down today — and how Reeco closes each one.

01
The Problem

Ottimate Is a Standalone AP Tool

Ottimate handles AP as a separate point solution — a premium spend that isn't connected to purchasing and isn't as deeply integrated with M3 and ProfitSword. Invoice coding, approvals, and payments stay siloed from how your properties actually buy.

The Answer

Invoices Coded in Seconds, Synced Straight to M3

Reeco captures every invoice and applies the correct GL code from your M3 chart of accounts, learning as it goes. Approved invoices sync to M3 in real time through a direct API — with advanced integration features available only to Reeco customers — alongside ProfitSword budget data and a clear path to one-click vendor payments through the Reeco, M3, and Paymerang partnership.

02
The Problem

BirchStreet's Punch-Out Model Slows Your Team Down

BirchStreet sends your team out to each supplier's own website to order, one vendor at a time. For a GM or department head who isn't a procurement professional, that's clunky and easy to avoid — which is exactly where adoption breaks down and off-system buying creeps back in.

The Answer

Every Supplier in One Marketplace, With Live Price Comparison

Reeco brings all your suppliers into one consolidated marketplace with live price comparison — search an item, see every supplier side by side, and order in a few taps, no hopping between vendor sites. Your GPO and HSM agreements are built right in, so the compliant, best-priced choice is the easy one. Approvals come through as mobile push notifications. It behaves like a consumer shopping experience, so the learning curve for GMs and department heads is hours, not days — and adoption sticks.

03
The Problem

Receiving Is Manual and Disconnected From the Order

Receiving happens on paper or in a separate step, disconnected from the original order. Missing items, short shipments, and credits slip through, and there's no clean tie back to the PO — so what you pay for isn't always what actually showed up at the property.

The Answer

Mobile Receiving, Backed by Reeco's Team

Your team checks in orders on the spot from the mobile app, right against the PO. Reeco's support and receiving services handle missing items and credits for you — recovering every credit owed — so discrepancies are caught at the dock instead of discovered at invoice time, and you only pay for what actually arrived.

04
The Problem

Purchasing and AP Live in Separate Systems

BirchStreet for purchasing and Ottimate for AP means two contracts, two logins, two support lines, and a gap between what was ordered and what was invoiced. Reconciling across them is manual, and no single system shows the full picture from PO to payment.

The Answer

One Connected Platform, PO to Payment

Purchasing, receiving, and AP automation in a single platform, on one login, with one support team. A true 3-way match lines up every order, receipt, and invoice automatically — so you pay the right amount, only for what arrived, and approved invoices flow straight to payment through Paymerang. The savings from moving off Ottimate help fund the purchasing module, so consolidating onto Reeco is both simpler and easier to justify.

05
The Problem

Inventory Counts Are Manual, If They Happen at All

Counts live on spreadsheets and clipboards and often get skipped, so shrinkage and over-ordering quietly eat margin. And because the numbers aren't current or trusted, they never feed back into purchasing — so ordering runs on gut instead of what's actually on the shelf.

The Answer

Fast Mobile Counts That Feed Smarter Ordering

Open the app, tap through the list, and the report is ready instantly — with always-current pricing. On your Full Service properties, accurate counts flow straight into purchasing, so orders match what you actually have on hand — less waste, fewer emergency buys, and tighter food cost.

Section 03  /  The Platform

What's Included.

Your proposal covers Purchasing & Receiving and AP Automation across the portfolio, plus Inventory on your Full Service properties. Culinary Management is a potential add-on when you're ready.

Core Module

Purchasing & Receiving

Full visibility and control across F&B, housekeeping, engineering, and service agreements. All your suppliers in one connected place — existing partnerships, pricing, and agreements honored. Mobile receiving lets your team check in orders on the spot; Reeco handles missing items and credits automatically.

Core Module

AP Automation

Every invoice — organized, AI-coded by line item, and synced to M3 as an unposted transaction. What used to take 7–10 minutes per invoice takes about 20 seconds. Enhanced integration with M3 and ProfitSword, plus coding that learns over time, so it gets faster and more accurate the longer you use it.

Full Service Module

Inventory

Today counts happen on spreadsheets or clipboards — if they happen at all — so shrinkage and over-ordering quietly erode margin, and there's no real count data to guide what you buy next. Reeco makes counts fast on mobile: open the app, tap through the list, and your report is ready instantly — with always-current pricing. Those counts feed straight back into purchasing, so ordering decisions are based on what you actually have on hand.

Potential Add-On

Culinary Management

Menus connected to recipes. Clear visibility into food cost by dish — what's making money, what's costing more than it should, and where to adjust before the next menu cycle. Integrates with your POS for live sales data, recipe costing, and automatic inventory depletion.

Section 08  /  FAQs

Questions We Expect — Answered Directly.

If something's missing, email Bill and we'll add it before the meeting.

"We already run BirchStreet and Ottimate. Why consolidate to Reeco?"
First, it's one connected platform for purchasing, receiving, and AP — one login, one support team, and no gap between what was ordered and what was invoiced. Second, BirchStreet uses a punch-out model where your team leaves to order on each supplier's site; Reeco is a consolidated marketplace with live price comparison across every vendor. Third, M3 has waived all integration fees for Reeco — the only procurement platform with that status — and we add enhanced ProfitSword forecast integration. And the savings from moving off Ottimate offset a good portion of the purchasing cost.
"How hard is implementation across 59 properties?"
Reeco does not require a dedicated full-time resource from your team. AP is the simplest part — the entire portfolio can be onboarded for AP in under a week. The M3 integration takes no effort on your side; it just needs a signature to grant permission. Most of the work for purchasing — configuring suppliers and product data — is handled by Reeco's onboarding team on the back end. A full plan specific to InnVentures, including property sequencing and timeline, is what we'll walk through at the implementation plan review.
"Will the M3 and ProfitSword integration work for our setup?"
Yes. Reeco and M3 have a direct, real-time API integration — the only procurement platform for which M3 has waived integration fees. Your GL codes and suppliers pull into Reeco automatically, and invoices approved in Reeco sync back to M3 as unposted transactions for your AP team to post. ProfitSword forecasts feed in so purchase orders are measured against forecast before they're submitted.
"What about adoption at the property level?"
Reeco is built for the user who isn't a procurement professional. It behaves like a consumer shopping experience — search an item, see prices from all your suppliers side by side, submit a PO in a few taps. Approvals come through as push notifications on your phone, and receiving happens in the same mobile app. No hopping between supplier portals like a punch-out model — the learning curve is hours, not days.
"How is our data protected?"
Reeco is SOC 2 Type II certified. The full audit report is available upon request — email billg@reeco.com and we'll send it within 24 hours.
